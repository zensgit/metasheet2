## Intro / scope

This document lists **small, concrete humanization micro-improvements** — single fields, toggles, defaults, copy-actions, inline affordances, and confirmation/notification touches — that close the *politeness/ergonomics* gap between MetaSheet attendance and the DingTalk admin experience. Each row traces to a **real manual screen** AND a **real route-inventory result** (a concrete `/api/attendance/...` path, or an honest "not found" where no route matches). This is deliberately **NOT** the big roadmap-arc work: per the benchmark ledger (`docs/development/attendance-dingtalk-benchmark-target-and-tracker-20260601.md`), the H2 MUST/SHOULD engines — **排班合规引擎, 未排班提醒, 打卡策略组, 加班↔调休, 假期过期管理, 自动对班**, plus the **C5 外发通知/负责人 fan-out** chain — are already shipped/closed (✅) arcs and are **out of scope** here, noted at the end. These rows are intentionally the "last mile" UX polish that sits *beside* (and on top of) those shipped arcs, sized S/M, never a new subsystem. The "MetaSheet today" column is anchored to the endpoint inventory, not keyword-grep counts (e.g. `shiftCompliance`/`enforceShiftEditWindow` exist but govern **schedule editing**, NOT attendance-*result* editing; `notification-deliveries` is the read-only C5 delivery log, and C5 fan-out today fires from *automatic producers* — there is no *manual/ad-hoc* admin trigger).
